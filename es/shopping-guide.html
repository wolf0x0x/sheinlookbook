Ofertas Nuevos Usuarios

First-time registration is the golden window for maximum discounts. Follow these steps to stack multiple new-user perks.

01

Register

Download the App or register via web with email. The system will automatically issue a first-order exclusive discount.

02

Claim Coupon

Go to "My Assets - Coupons" and confirm your new-user large no-threshold or tiered coupon is active.

03

Stack & Checkout

At checkout, the system auto-matches the best discount combo. Make sure "new user free shipping" is checked.
